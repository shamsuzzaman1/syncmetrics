📋 How to use your MCP server in Claude
1
Click "Connect with Meta" above to link your Meta Ads account.
2
Copy your personal MCP URL from the blue box at the top of this page.
3
Open Claude.ai → Settings → Integrations and click Add MCP Server.
4
Paste your personal MCP URL, name it SyncMetrics and click Save.
5
Start a new chat in Claude and ask: What are my Meta ad accounts?
