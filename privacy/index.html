Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2025

SyncMetrics ("we", "us", or "our") operates syncmetrics.io. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. What We Collect

When you use SyncMetrics, we collect:

  • Account information — your email address and encrypted password (via Supabase Auth)
  • Ad platform tokens — OAuth access tokens from Meta Ads, encrypted at rest using AES-256. We never store your Meta password.
  • Ad data — campaign names, spend, impressions, clicks, and other performance metrics fetched on your behalf from Meta's API. We do not permanently store this data; it is fetched in real time and returned directly to you.
  • Usage logs — standard server access logs (IP address, endpoint, timestamp) retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and manage your account
  • To connect to Meta Ads on your behalf and retrieve your ad performance data
  • To display your data in Claude (via MCP) and Looker Studio (via our connector)
  • To send transactional emails (e.g. account confirmation)

We do not sell your data. We do not use your ad data for advertising or profiling.

3. Meta Ads Data

SyncMetrics connects to Meta's Marketing API using permissions you explicitly grant. We request only the permissions needed:

  • ads_read — to read your ad account performance data
  • pages_show_list — to list your Facebook Pages

Your Meta access token is stored encrypted (AES-256 / Fernet). You can revoke our access at any time from your Facebook App Settings or by disconnecting from your SyncMetrics dashboard.

4. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

  • Supabase — database and authentication provider (data stored in their EU/US infrastructure)
  • Meta (Facebook) — we call Meta's API on your behalf using your token
  • Render — our server hosting provider

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

5. Data Retention

  • Your account and encrypted tokens are kept until you delete your account
  • Ad performance data is not stored — it is fetched live each time you request it
  • Server logs are retained for up to 30 days

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Disconnect Meta — click "Disconnect" on your dashboard to delete your Meta token immediately
  • Delete account — email us at support@syncmetrics.io to have all your data permanently deleted
  • Via Facebook — removing SyncMetrics from your Facebook App Settings triggers automatic deletion of your Meta token from our systems

7. Security

We protect your data using:

  • AES-256 encryption for all stored access tokens
  • HTTPS for all data in transit
  • Supabase Row Level Security to prevent cross-user data access
  • No permanent storage of ad performance data

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at support@syncmetrics.io.

9. Cookies

We use only essential session cookies required for authentication. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. We will notify you by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@syncmetrics.io.
